# JLIB Navigation Cases

## Scenario List

1. Identify a page in `Current Laws` and capture the `Showing the law from ... to Current` range.
2. Distinguish `Current` from `Current point-in-time` inside the same collection.
3. Distinguish `Pending` from enacted or current law.
4. Distinguish `Enacted` from official current consolidated text.
5. Distinguish `Archive (non-current)` from official current material.
6. Distinguish `Repealed` material from law still in force.
7. Recognise that `Translated` laws are unofficial reading aids.
8. Recognise that `Annotated` laws are contextual aids rather than current-law substitutes.
9. Recognise when a current-collection page is actually UK-extended material and needs special caution.
