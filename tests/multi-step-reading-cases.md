# Multi-Step Reading Cases

## Scenario List

1. Present-law question answered from current official Jersey legislation.
2. Historical question from 2021 requiring `Current point-in-time` rather than the latest current page.
3. Original-text question requiring `Enacted` rather than current consolidated text.
4. Pre-2019 historical question requiring `Archive` material with limitations stated.
5. `Pending` law that looks relevant but cannot answer a current-law question.
6. A translated French-law question that needs both the translation and the authoritative-source caution.
7. An annotated-law question that requires moving back to the operative provision.
8. UK-extended material requiring orientation, date control, and a stronger boundary warning.
9. A question where definitions and cross-references matter more than the face of the provision.
10. A question where legislation alone is not enough because facts or case law would matter.
