# Multi-Step Reading Cases

## Scenario List

1. A legislation question with no stable JLIB page yet, requiring `00 -> 01 -> 02` before substantive reading.
2. Present-law question answered from current official Jersey legislation.
3. Historical question from 2021 requiring `Current point-in-time` rather than the latest current page.
4. Original-text question requiring `Enacted` rather than current consolidated text.
5. Pre-2019 historical question requiring `Archive` material with limitations stated.
6. `Pending` law that looks relevant but cannot answer a current-law question.
7. A translated French-law question that needs both the translation and the authoritative-source caution.
8. An annotated-law question that requires moving back to the operative provision.
9. UK-extended material requiring orientation, date control, and a stronger boundary warning.
10. A question where definitions and cross-references matter more than the face of the provision and can go from `05 -> 07`.
11. A question where a Jersey-wide Interpretation Law default is outcome-relevant and the chain should go `05 -> 06 -> 07`.
12. A question where legislation alone is not enough because facts or case law would matter.
