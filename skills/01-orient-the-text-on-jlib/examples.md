# Examples

## Example 1

Input: A JLIB page headed with an instrument title and a visible "pending" label.

Use: Record that the text is pending material, identify the provision in view, and warn against treating it as already in force.

## Example 2

Input: A copied article from JLIB with no surrounding context.

Use: Ask first for the source page or collection label, because the extract alone may hide whether it is current or historical.

## Example 3

Input: A translated JLIB page for a Jersey law.

Use: Note that the translation may assist reading, but flag the need to confirm the operative source if authority matters.
