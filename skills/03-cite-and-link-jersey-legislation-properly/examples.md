# Examples

## Example 1

Input: A current JLIB page showing Article 7 of a Law.

Use: Cite the instrument title plus `Article 7`, and include that it is the current text if that matters to the task.

## Example 2

Input: A Schedule paragraph in non-current material.

Use: Cite the instrument, schedule, and paragraph, and mark clearly that the material is historical or non-current.

## Example 3

Input: A user refers only to "page 4 of the legislation".

Use: Convert the reference into a provision-level citation if possible, or say the provision has not yet been identified.
