# Walkthrough: When To Stop

## Scenario

A user asks whether a person would definitely have a legal right or liability under a provision, but the text is ambiguous and context is thin.

## Suggested Skill Sequence

1. `01-orient-the-text-on-jlib`
   Confirm what text is being used.
2. `02-find-the-right-version-and-date`
   Ensure the version is even the right one.
3. `04-read-a-provision-as-logic`
   Show the competing limbs or uncertainty points.
4. `05-apply-definitions-and-cross-references`
   Resolve any immediate dependencies.
5. `06-apply-non-obvious-background-rules`
   Note whether broader interpretive context may matter.
6. `07-boundary-check-legislation-alone-or-not`
   Stop short of a definitive answer if legislation alone is not enough.

## Safe Outcome

The correct result may be a limited answer: what the provision appears to say, what remains unresolved, and what extra material would be needed before going further.
