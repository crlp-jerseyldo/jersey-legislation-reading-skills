# Walkthrough: Current Law

## Scenario

A user asks: `What does this Jersey provision say now?`

The JLIB page is in `Current Laws` and states `Showing the law from 11 January 2024 to Current`.

## Suggested Skill Sequence

1. `01-orient-the-text-on-jlib`
   Record `Current`, official consolidated status, the page date range, and the provision in view.
2. `02-find-the-right-version-and-date`
   Match the question to present law and confirm that `Current` is the right source state.
3. `03-cite-and-link-jersey-legislation-properly`
   Produce a provision-level citation that preserves the page date range and direct JLIB link.
4. `04-read-a-provision-as-logic`
   Break the provision into rule, limbs, and exceptions.
5. `05-apply-definitions-and-cross-references`
   Pull in definitions, same-instrument links, and any parent-enactment meaning if relevant.
6. `06-apply-non-obvious-background-rules`
   Check Jersey-wide defaults only if one of the maintained triggers arises.
7. `07-boundary-check-legislation-alone-or-not`
   Separate the text summary from any applied legal conclusion.

## Safe Outcome

Provide a cautious provision-level summary tied to the official current page and its stated date range. Do not move from that summary to a fact-sensitive legal answer unless the limits have been checked explicitly.
