# Walkthrough: Current Law

## Scenario

A user asks: "What does this Jersey provision say now?"

## Suggested Skill Sequence

1. `01-orient-the-text-on-jlib`
   Record the instrument, collection, and status marker.
2. `02-find-the-right-version-and-date`
   Confirm that the task is about present law and that the text appears current.
3. `03-cite-and-link-jersey-legislation-properly`
   Identify the exact article or paragraph being discussed.
4. `04-read-a-provision-as-logic`
   Break the provision into rule, conditions, and exceptions.
5. `05-apply-definitions-and-cross-references`
   Check unresolved terms and linked provisions.
6. `07-boundary-check-legislation-alone-or-not`
   State what can be said from the legislation alone and whether further context is needed.

## Safe Outcome

Provide a provision-level summary tied to the current text, while still marking any unresolved definitions or interpretation issues.
